# GTM Claude Skills

A curated collection of Claude skills built for **B2B SaaS go-to-market teams**: helping marketers, sales enablement leads, and GTM operators work faster and smarter with LLMs.

## What are Claude skills?

Skills are pre-packaged instructions that extend Claude's capabilities for specific, recurring tasks. Install a skill once, and Claude follows a consistent, expert-level methodology every time you invoke it: no re-prompting needed. 
Best practice: give feedback to Claude every time it uses the skill, and ask it to update the skill to your preference.

→ Learn how to install skills: [claude.ai/skills](https://claude.ai)

## Skills

### 🎯 `client-slides`
Produce polished, client-facing slide decks to MBB consulting standards.

Enforces action titles that pass the "So What" test, pyramid principle structure, MECE logic, smart brevity, and a final audit checklist, so every deck tells a tight, persuasive story.

**Best for:** Executive Business Reviews, meeting with a client's VP , partner decks, sales proposals...

---

### 🛠️ `internal-slides`
Build internal, work-in-progress slide decks the consulting way.

Guides you through ghost deck creation, SCR/SCQA storyline frameworks, slide stickers for content maturity, and MECE logic at every level, so you align your team around an argument before investing in polish.

**Best for:** Strategy reviews, internal alignment decks, leadership updates, org proposals.

---
